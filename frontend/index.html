Welcome to SignBridge

Turn hand gestures into English and Acholi text in real time, powered by MediaPipe Holistic and a Transformer AI model.

📷

Position Your Camera

Hold your hand 30–60 cm from the camera in good light. Keep your whole upper body visible so pose landmarks can be tracked alongside your hands.

🟢

Start Translating

Tap Start Translating and perform your sign in front of the camera. Every 30 frames the AI model predicts the gesture and the translation appears automatically.

🌍

Read the Translation

Results appear below the camera in both English and Acholi (Luo). Tap the copy icon to share the text, or tap Games in the navigation to practise Uganda Sign Language interactively.

Quick Help

  • 🟢Start Translating — begins the live 225-float landmark stream to the AI model.
  • 🔴Stop Translating — stops the stream and ends the session.
  • 📶The dot in the header shows WebSocket connection health (green = live).
  • 💡Stay within 60 cm, well-lit, with your full torso visible for best accuracy.
  • 🤖The AI triggers a prediction every 30 frames — results appear automatically.
  • 🌐Tap the globe icon to toggle the Acholi translation on/off.
  • ⚙️Tap Settings in the bottom nav to change the backend server URL when hosting remotely.
  • 🎮Tap Games to practise Uganda Sign Language with SignMaster.
